# Penn Mutual (penn-mutual)

The Penn Mutual Life Insurance Company is a mutual life insurer founded in 1847 and headquartered in Horsham, Pennsylvania, making it one of the oldest mutual life insurance companies in the United States. Owned by its policyholders, Penn Mutual offers life insurance and annuity products distributed primarily through career and independent financial professionals, and operates through a family of companies including Vantis Life Insurance Company, Hornor Townsend & Kent (HTK) broker-dealer, and the Penn Insurance and Annuity Company (PIA). Penn Mutual divested its long-held wealth management subsidiary, Janney Montgomery Scott, to KKR in 2024.

## APIs

No public APIs have been documented. Penn Mutual does not expose a public developer portal, OpenAPI specifications, SDKs, or a GitHub presence with public repositories. Producer, advisor, and partner integrations are handled through private channels and direct business relationships with financial professionals affiliated with Penn Mutual and its subsidiaries.

## Family of Companies

- **[Vantis Life Insurance Company](https://www.vantislife.com)** — Direct-to-consumer and bank/credit-union channel life insurer; Penn Mutual subsidiary since 2016.
- **[Hornor, Townsend & Kent, LLC (HTK)](https://www.htk.com)** — Wholly-owned full-service broker-dealer and registered investment adviser.
- **The Penn Insurance and Annuity Company (PIA)** — Wholly-owned subsidiary issuing life and annuity products through institutional and independent channels.
- **[Janney Montgomery Scott](https://www.janney.com)** *(divested)* — Sold to funds managed by KKR & Co. in 2024; no longer a Penn Mutual subsidiary.
